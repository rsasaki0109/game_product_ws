# Roll Ball Rush

## One-line
Players race inside giant rolling balls and try to control speed through a downhill course.

## Why it works
- Very fast to understand
- Good motion even in a simple map
- Easy to make clips from near-miss turns and last-second boosts

## Core Loop
1. Spawn inside a transparent ball shell
2. Roll through a downhill lane
3. Use `Boost` on long straights
4. Use `Brake` before corners or narrow platforms
5. Touch checkpoints to secure respawns
6. Cross the finish gate

## MVP Rules
- 1 to 8 players
- 75 second round
- Checkpoints reset you when you fall
- Finish order decides winners
- No items, no combat

## Controls
- `Q`: Boost
- `E`: Brake
- `WASD`: Steer

## Map Shape
- Start platform
- First downhill ramp
- Right-side landing
- Long second ramp
- Cross-lane turn
- Final ramp into finish gate

## Expansion Ideas
- Breakable glass shortcuts
- Bumper pads
- Mud zones that fight boost timing
- Team relay mode
